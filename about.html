About Swarmcraft!

Swarmcraft, is a small Minecraft group. It's founders are "VoxKnight" and "Materialize127". They created Swarmcraft to invite people they liked or thought were special! Since then it's lost and gained people. Swarmcraft is a place of good thoughts. We all talk to each other, we all respect each other, and we play Minecraft!


The main purpose of Swarmcraft is to play Minecraft and have a jolly good time with friends!
